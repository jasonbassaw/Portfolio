Content

Home page

The home page will be the page holding the other pages. It will be like the primary point that will serve as a hub linking to the various site or pages of my portfolio online site. it will come with a portfolio logo and a hero banner that will quickly get the attention of visitors. These features of visuals are to give visitors an immersive experience to stay in mi site to get to know me more. It will have the navigation bar which will contain and guide visitors to the links of my About Me page and my Work Experience page. It will also contain the Contact Me which will hold my primarily contact details such as my phone number, email address etc. It will also have the blog link also. It is going to give a brief statement that will summarize my background and what is there to be seen on the page. It will go ahead and include the footer which will establish a strong online presence by showcasing my social media platforms, such as Instagram, LinkedIn and Facebook. These platforms will allow visitors to easily connect with me, getting to exploring my personality, my interest, skills, educational background as well as professional journey etc.

About Me

The about me page will contain information about me, my skills, my achievements. personality, and my professional background. This page will be a medium for visitors to learn more about me, and to get a sense of who I am as a person and as a professional. I will start of by briefing visitors about my education, I will mention the degrees and certificates or some trainings that I have completed. I will show my profile picture at the background to give the visitors a face and my name boldly written aside. I will also include a short section of my interest and hobbies, as that can depict my personality to the visitors. I will finish off by giving a brief background of my qualifications and skills in a relevant field.

Images for the Page 2

Work experience

The work experience page is where I am going to showcase into details my professional journey. I will start of by listing my work history starting with the most recent position to the least recent. For all the roles, I will include the job title, the company name and the date of employment as well as the description of my responsibilities and some key bullet points. There is also going to be a section dedicated to talks about my volunteer work which will be highlighting and explaining my involvement and the skills that are being achieved during that timeline. This will show all my experience that I acquired from that. One of the important things that will be on my work experience page is going to be the showcasing of my relevant skills and key competence gained over the years of my professional journey both technical and hard skill as well as soft skills. It will display a background picture after every section. The page will be visually engaging, every section of the page will include a background picture that will be depicting every aspect of my work experience journey, It will help it to be more engaging and interesting to get to know more about me.
